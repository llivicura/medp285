Timeline

Before we delve deeper into the events that transpired during quarantine, I would first like to present a timeline highlighting events that led up to this global pandemic. This will provide a brief overview of the virus and also serve as a firm reminder of the amount of time our country's leaders had to prepare for this impending emergency.

  • First Signs

    China alerts WHO of increasing number of pneumonia cases

    December 31

  • Virus Identified

    Chinese authorities identify virus causing pneumonia-like symptoms. It's identified as a new type of coronavirus (novel coronavirus)

    January 7

  • Wuhan Under Quarantine

    Wuhan placed under quarantine in an effort to contain mysterious virus

    January 23

  • Public Health Emergency

    WHO declares the coronavirus outbreak a Public Health Emergency of International Concern

    January 30

  • First American Death

    A person in California dies from coronavirus

    February 7

  • Spike in Italy

    Coronavirus cases begin to exponentially rise in Italy

    February 21

  • Lockdown in Italy

    All residents of Italy are placed on lockdown

    March 8

  • Pandemic

    WHO declares the outbreak a pandemic

    March 11

  • New York Shuts Down

    Governor Cuomo orders all nonessential businesses in the state to close and nonessential workers to stay home

    March 22
