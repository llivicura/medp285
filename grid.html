Music is an art form, created by a combination of different sounds. Described as a universal language, music ignores language barriers and can appease to any person in the world.

Today, many genre of music exists, ranging from electronic to the rising popularity of K-pop. While some may prefer one over the other, one can't really know what they like until they give it a try.

For this assignment, the theme is music, more specifically an introduction to 4 genres of music that I am currently listening to and hope to share with others. The site is constructed as a grid and I have nested a grid within showcasing some samples of songs that pertain to the genres that I'm sharing. The four genres that you will see are: heavy metal, chillwave, K-pop, and spanish rock.
